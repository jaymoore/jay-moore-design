02 / Each step

Each bar is a step. Width is how long it took.

Six steps from call to delivered SMS: receive, transcribe, classify, wait, send, deliver. The wait between classify and send — drawn in blue, about two seconds — is the longest. Hide the timing in a log and nobody knows why the system feels slow. Show it as a bar chart and the bottleneck is obvious.
